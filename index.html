About Me

About Me

I've been diving into cosplay since 2018, and it's been quite the journey. Along the way, I've soaked up heaps of knowledge from experienced cosplayers who were kind enough to share their wisdom with me. It's been amazing to learn the ins and outs of this creative world.

One of the coolest parts of cosplay has been the chance to meet tons of awesome people at anime and comic conventions. There's something special about bonding over shared fandoms and geeking out together! The con-after party's are always really fun!

When it comes to cosplaying, I'm all about bringing to life characters that really mean something to me. Whether it's a childhood hero or a recent favorite, stepping into their shoes is always a thrill. And you better believe I'm always game for a fun photoshoot or strutting my stuff on the catwalk!

Looking ahead, I'm super excited about the prospect of teaming up with other talented cosplayers. There's so much creativity out there, and I can't wait to see what awesome collaborations we can come up with. Let's dive into this world of cosplay together and make some unforgettable memories!
